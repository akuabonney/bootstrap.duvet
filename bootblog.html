Ghanaians Love Food

Post by Awurakua Bonney.
Food Ipsum

Ghanaians love food. Period. It has nothing to do with being foodians or anything, we really just have a deep appreciation for our various amazing cuisines and we do not hide it. Why should we? And what’s the better way to enjoy our wonderful local food if it’s not indoors in our warm clothes on a rainy day. With the way the Ghanaian weather is set up, we tend to joke that the cold rainy day is perfect for couples.
